Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a library of free and open-source fonts that you can use in your web projects. It offers a vast collection of fonts from various categories, including serif, sans-serif, display, handwriting, and more. By using Google Fonts, you can enhance the visual appeal of your website and make it more unique and engaging. You can easily integrate Google Fonts into your website using CSS.
