

In WD,accessibility refers to designing and developing websites, tools, and applications in a way that 
ensures they can be used by people with a wide range of abilities and disabilities. 
This includes considerations for users with visual, auditory, motor, cognitive, or neurological impairments.
 Accessibility aims to remove barriers and provide an inclusive user experience for all.

 WHY ??
 Creating accessible websites promotes inclusivity, complies with legal requirements, improves usability for all users, 
 expands audience reach, and enhances SEO and performance. 
 It benefits people with disabilities, aging populations, users with temporary impairments, and even those in challenging environments.
