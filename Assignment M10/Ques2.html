Explanation of BEM Naming:

  • Block: "content-section" groups related paragraphs as a cohesive unit.
  • Element: "__paragraph" identifies the paragraphs as elements of the block.
  • Modifier: "--intro", "--info", "--warning", and "--note" denote specific styles or purposes for the paragraphs.
